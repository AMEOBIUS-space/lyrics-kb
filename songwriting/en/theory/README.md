# Songwriting — EN · Theory

English-language songwriting theory (Pattison / Berklee lineage and beyond).

Planned:

- **Prosody** — matching lyric stress to melodic stress; stable vs unstable line endings.
- **Rhyme types** — perfect, family, additive, subtractive, assonance, consonance; rhyme scheme and its emotional weight.
- **Song structure** — verse / pre-chorus / chorus / bridge; sectional contrast; the "where are we" map.
- **Meter & phrasing** — line length, number of stresses, front-heavy vs back-heavy lines.
- **Hooks & titles** — the title as center of gravity; hook placement.
