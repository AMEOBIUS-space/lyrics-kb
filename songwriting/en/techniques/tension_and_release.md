# Tension & release — expectation management

Songs are delayed resolution. Tension without release exhausts; release without tension bores.

---

## 1. Levels of tension

| Level | Tools |
|---|---|
| **Sonic** | dissonance, filter close, rising pitch, snare rolls, silence |
| **Harmonic** | withheld tonic, loop on IV/V, pedal under changing chords |
| **Lyrical** | unanswered questions, unstable endings, incomplete info |
| **Narrative** | clock, risk, secret, distance between want and have |
| **Formal** | delay chorus entry; fake-out pre-chorus |

Writers own lyrical, narrative, formal — and must leave air for sonic/harmonic.

---

## 2. Unstable → stable (lyric)

From prosody craft:
- stack unstable line endings in pre-chorus
- land stable on title

Example shape:
```
verse:   detail / detail / lean
pre:     lean / lean / lean harder
chorus:  land / land / title land
```

---

## 3. Information delay

Don't dump the whole story in V1.

| Section | Reveal |
|---|---|
| V1 | situation + itch |
| Chorus | emotional claim (title) |
| V2 | cause / complication |
| Bridge | cost or confession |
| Final chorus | claim re-heard with new weight |

**Withhold the name, the date, or the decision** until the ear needs it.

---

## 4. Repetition as tension

Exact repeat can:
- **Release** — mantra comfort (*"it's alright"* ×3 after chaos)
- **Tension** — stuck thought (*"I'm fine"* while detail proves otherwise)

Control which by context and performance direction.

Variation on last repeat = micro-release or twist-knife.

---

## 5. The "almost chorus"

Pre-chorus that sounds like chorus but denies the title = strong tension. Deliver title only when arrangement opens.

---

## 6. Bridge as pressure valve or deep dive

Two bridge modes:
1. **Valve** — thinner, quieter, private (release before final lift)
2. **Dive** — denser truth (increase tension, then explode into chorus)

Pick one; don't do both halfway.

---

## 7. Checklist

- [ ] Listener can feel a climb into the chorus?
- [ ] Something is still unknown after V1?
- [ ] Title landing feels like arrival, not another line?
- [ ] Bridge changes pressure (up or down) on purpose?
- [ ] Final chorus earns its repeat?
