# Cross · Narrative arc

Language-neutral song story map.

## Minimal spine

1. **Where** — one filmable place
2. **Who** — voice with a body and a want
3. **Itch** — what is wrong / unfinished
4. **Turn** — new fact only V2/bridge can hold
5. **Cost** — visible remainder after loss
6. **Exit** — action or residue, not moral

## Section mapping

| Section | Narrative job |
|---|---|
| Verse 1 | where + who + itch |
| Chorus | thesis / title claim |
| Verse 2 | complication (non-paraphrase) |
| Bridge | cost or reframe |
| Final chorus | thesis with new weight |
| Outro | residue / action |

## Anti-patterns

- V2 synonym loop
- Chorus checklist of virtues
- Outro comfort speech
- Thesis never lands on a stable line
