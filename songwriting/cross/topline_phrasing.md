# Cross · Topline phrasing (lyric constraints)

Lyric rules that serve melody regardless of language.

## Vowels and sustain

- Long notes want open vowels (a/o/ah/ay)
- Avoid packing consonant clusters on sustained pitches
- Title syllable under the money note must be singable

## Breath and BPM

| BPM | Phrase bias |
|---|---|
| 70–90 | longer lines OK |
| 110–130 | shorter phrases, more rests |
| 140+ | fragments / chants |

If a line cannot be spoken in one breath at target BPM — split it.

## Stress

- Meaning words on strong beats
- Particles off the peak
- Same instinct as EN prosody / RU тоника count

## Checklist

- [ ] Title singable on the peak note?
- [ ] Breath possible at BPM?
- [ ] No cluster-chew on sustains?
- [ ] Phrase lengths vary with section energy?
