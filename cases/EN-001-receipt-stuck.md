## Concept

Plant lot after late shift. Rain on asphalt, not neon veins. Hero checks the car, finds the receipt from the machine shop glued to skin. Hook is money + body, not therapy. "You" is the foreman/ex who left the lot early — one physical act. Outro: keys, door, no apology.
